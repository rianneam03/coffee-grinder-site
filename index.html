Our Story

Brewed with love, served with heart

Welcome to The Coffee Grinder, where every cup tells a story and every visit feels like coming home. We're not just another coffee shop—we're a family-owned haven where Trevor and his family pour their hearts into everything we create.

Each family member brings their own magic to our menu. From hand-crafted cookies and delicate pastries to rich cakes and creamy cheesecakes, every treat is made with love and a secret family recipe or two. We believe in doing things the right way: real whole cream in your coffee, beans from local roasters who know their craft, and ingredients that honor both tradition and taste.

This isn't corporate coffee. This is the kind of place where baristas remember your order, where the art on the walls is made by your neighbors, and where a Tuesday morning can turn into an impromptu conversation about life, art, or the best way to make a latte.

Fun fact:

We're an Amsterdam-style coffee house right here in Jenks, Oklahoma. It's a little unexpected, a little quirky, and exactly what makes us special.
